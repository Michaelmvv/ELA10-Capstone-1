Using Technology

I am using my essay rewrites from both AP History and ELA to demonstrate my ability to use technology and digital media strategically and capably. In these assignments I take essays that I did not do well on and rewrite them on my computer. In AP World all that needs to be rewritten is a paragraph and a thesis. In language arts the whole essay must be rewritten. This demonstrates my growth in uses of technology because I became more proficient in web applications such as google docs. I also became more informed about how typed essays should be written. They should be double spaced, 12pt font, Times New Roman, have a header, and so on. Another example of this is this capstone itself. Here I am using my knowledge of HTML to create a nice webpage to present my information. Currently I give myself an A in this area. I give myself this grade because I believe that I have effectively used technology to enhance my learning experience. My goal for next semester is to continue to use technology to enhance my learning experience because it both helps me learn and make the projects interesting. Working digitaly makes the information easier for me to remember. Another goal is to make an even better capstone at the end of next semester.
