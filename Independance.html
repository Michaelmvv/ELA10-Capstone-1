Demonstrating Independence

I am using the warm ups that I do almost every day in ELA to demonstrate my independence. In the warmups I respond to a prompt put on the overhead projector. The warmups require one to independently answer them. The warmups require me to do many things. Some require making a claim, some require using complex vocabulary, and others require me to find key points of a text or image. This assignment demonstrates my growth because as the year has progressed my warmups have gotten better and more accurate. Further indicating my growth in independence is that I do not talk while I do my warm ups, which is independent. Another thing showing my independance is my dislike for working in groups. I find that it is easier and faster to work alone on projects than work with others. Currently, I give myself an A in this area because I can construct effective arguments, I can convey intricate information, I acquire new vocabulary, and I have command of standard English. My goal for next semester is to continue being independent because I work better alone. I also hope to become a better speller because my bad spelling is somewhat embarrassing.
