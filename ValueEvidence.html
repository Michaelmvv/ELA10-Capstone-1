I am using the Imperialism/Colonialism performance task to demonstrate my ability to value evidence. In this task we had to read multiple documents and then write an essay about it. In this case it was about whether imperialism or colonialism was beneficial to or detrimental to the places it occurred in. In this assignment I had to cite specific evidence from the texts provided to prove my point. This demonstrates my growth because I learned that I had trouble finding evidence to support my ideas. This led me to work harder on gathering good evidence in later projects, which I did better on. Currently I give myself a C in this area. I give myself this grade because I still have trouble making clear reasoning and finding relevant evidence. My goal for next semester is to improve my ability to find and use relevant information in my essays because it will help me bring my grades up.
